Goal

Be the first player to form a line of 5 of your marbles either horizontally, vertically, or diagonally.

This is the Wood League. The game is played in a 1v1 duel on a 6x6 grid made of 4 rotating 3x3 blocks.

  Rules

On your turn, you must perform exactly two actions:

  1. Place a marble on any empty space on the grid.
  2. Rotate a 3x3 block 90 degrees either left (counter-clockwise) or right (clockwise).

Blocks are numbered 0 to 3, and coordinates x, y from 0 to 5 as shown below:

If multiple players form a line of 5 simultaneously, it's a draw between those players.

  Game Input/Output

Input for one game turn

Line 1: size, the size of the board (6).

Next size lines: strings representing the grid state. A . means empty. A number 0-1 represents a player's marble.

Output for one game turn
A single line containing 4 space-separated values: x y block dir
x, y: Coordinates to place your marble.
block: The ID of the block you wish to rotate (0 to 3).
dir: The direction to rotate (L or R).
