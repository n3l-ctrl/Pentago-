This is a league based challenge.

For this challenge, multiple leagues for the same game are available. Once you have proven your worth against the Boss, you will access the higher league.

Bronze League: The training wheels are off. The board has expanded to a massive 9x9 board, and you are now facing up to 3 opponents simultaneously in a free-for-all!

  Goal

Be the first player to form a line of 5 of your marbles either horizontally, vertically, or diagonally.
This puzzle is based on the board game Pentago.

  Rules

On your turn, you must perform exactly two actions:

  1. Place a marble on any empty space on the board.
  2. Rotate a 3x3 block 90 degrees either left (counter-clockwise) or right (clockwise).

Blocks are numbered 0 to 8, and coordinates x, y from 0 to 8 as shown below:

Draws: If multiple players form a line of 5 simultaneously, it is a draw between those players. If the board is completely filled with no lines of 5, the game ends in a global draw for all active players.


Victory Conditions
  • Form a line of 5 marbles of your color horizontally, vertically, or diagonally.
Defeat Conditions
  • Your program times out, crashes, or outputs an invalid command.

  Game Input/Output

Initialization Input

Line 1: playerCount, the number of players in the game.

Line 2: myId, your player ID (0 to 3).

Input for one game turn

Line 1: size, the size of the board (9).

Next size lines: strings representing the board state. A . means empty. A number 0-3 represents a player's marble.

Output for one game turn
A single line containing 4 space-separated values: x y block dir
x, y: Coordinates to place your marble.
block: The ID of the block you wish to rotate (0 to 8).
dir: The direction to rotate (L or R).

Example: 2 1 0 R (Places marble at 2,1 and rotates block 0 right)
Constraints
Response time for first turn ≤ 1000 ms
Response time for one turn ≤ 50 ms
