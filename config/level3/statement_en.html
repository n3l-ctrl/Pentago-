Goal

Be the first player to form a line of 5 of your marbles either horizontally, vertically, or diagonally.

Welcome to the final Arena! You are playing on a massive 9x9 grid against an opponent.

A new game-changing mechanic has been introduced: SWAP. You can now physically exchange the positions of two adjacent 3x3 blocks instead of rotating a block!

  Rules

On your turn, you must perform exactly two actions:

  1. Place a marble on any empty space on the grid.
  2. Manipulate the board using ONE of the following methods:
    • Rotate a 3x3 block 90 degrees either left or right.
    • Swap the physical positions of two adjacent 3x3 blocks (blocks sharing an edge).

  Game Input/Output

Input for one game turn

Line 1: size, the size of the board (9).

Next size lines: strings representing the grid state. A . means empty. A number 0-1 represents a player's marble.

Output for one game turn
Option A (Rotate): x y block dir
dir is L or R.

Option B (Swap): x y SWAP b1 b2
b1 and b2 are the IDs of the two adjacent blocks you wish to swap.
