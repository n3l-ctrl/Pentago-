  Goal

Be the first player to form a line of 5 of your marbles either horizontally, vertically, or diagonally.
This puzzle is based on the official board game Pentago.

  Rules

On your turn, you must perform exactly two actions:

  1. Place a marble on any empty space on the board.
  2. Rotate a 3x3 block 90 degrees either left (counter-clockwise) or right (clockwise).
  3. Rotate a 3x3 block 90 degrees either left (counter-clockwise) or right (clockwise).
  4. Manipulate the board using ONE of the following methods:
    • Rotate a 3x3 block 90 degrees either left or right.
    • Swap the physical positions of two adjacent 3x3 blocks (blocks sharing an edge).
  5. Manipulate the board using ONE of the following methods:
    • Rotate a 3x3 block 90 degrees either left or right.
    • Swap the physical positions of two adjacent 3x3 blocks (blocks sharing an edge).

Draws: If multiple players form a line of 5 simultaneously, it is a draw between those players. If the board is completely filled with no lines of 5, the game ends in a global draw for all active players.

Output for one game turn
A single line containing 4 space-separated values: x y block dir
x, y: Coordinates to place your marble.
block: The ID of the block you wish to rotate (0 to 3).
dir: The direction to rotate (L or R).

Example: 2 1 0 R (Places marble at 2,1 and rotates block 0 right)
